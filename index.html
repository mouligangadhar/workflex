Our #mission
As a nonprofit organization, People Centered Lighthouse remains apolitical, fostering a cultural and socioeconomic focus to support the underprivileged and underserved. Our belief in the good news of Jesus Christ propels our mission, where all tax-deductible donations directly benefit our community through promoting culture, education, training, coaching, and mentoring.
